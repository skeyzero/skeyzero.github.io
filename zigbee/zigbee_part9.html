In the case of a child ZigBee Router, the NWK address will not be reused.当一个有子设备得设备离开网络，它得子设备将保持在原来网络。 尽管“NLME-LEAVE.request” 提供多个参数。但ZigBee 2007 (as well as Texas Instrument’s current implementation) 限制用户使用这些参数. 现在(“RemoveChildren”, “Rejoin”, and “Silent”) 应该设置为缺省值。“ZDO_ProcessMgmtLeaveReq”.
